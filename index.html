FAQ

What tools and technologies do you use in your workflow?

My workflow includes tools like Visual Studio Code, Git for version control and collaboration, as well as various debugging and testing tools. I am also constantly learning and exploring new technologies that can improve my efficiency and work quality.

How do you approach performance optimization in your frontend projects?

Performance optimization is crucial in web development. I employ practices such as image optimization, file minification, and the use of lazy loading techniques to enhance loading speed and efficiency of the websites I develop.

Do you offer maintenance or support services after completing a frontend project?

Yes, I provide maintenance and support services after project completion. I am available for updates, bug fixes, or any assistance the client might need to ensure the optimal functioning of the website.
